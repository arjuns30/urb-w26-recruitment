WHO ARE WE?

Who Are We

URB Consulting is the fastest-growing student-led consulting organization at the University of Michigan, focused on companies at the intersection of the digital and physical worlds—any industry that impacts the built environment including automotive, aviation, energy, architecture and so many more!

We work with innovative startups, government agencies, and industry-leading tech companies to deliver high-impact projects spanning technical implementation, design analysis, and strategic decision making. Through this work, URB members develop entrepreneurial, technical and business acumen while solving real-world problems for companies in internship style projects to build your resume!

THE URB COMMUNITY

The URB Community

URB is more than a consulting club—it's a community professionally and socially. Members support one another through:

  • Professional workshops and skills training
  • Treks to cities around the country
  • Mentorship and resume development
  • Collaborative project teams and social events

Our members grow together, form close bonds, and gain hands-on experience that translates directly to immense personal and career growth.
